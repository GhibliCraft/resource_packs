# Contributing to GhibliCraft Resource Packs

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

This repository is maintained for the GhibliCraft project and is subject to project-specific review standards. Because this is an unofficial fan project, contributions may be accepted, edited, declined, or removed at the maintainers’ discretion.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository.
2. Create a branch for your change.
3. Make your changes.
4. Submit a pull request with a clear description of what changed.

Please keep pull requests focused and easy to review.

## What You May Submit

Contributions may include:

- texture improvements
- model improvements
- bug fixes
- compatibility fixes
- organizational cleanup
- documentation updates
- screenshots approved by the maintainers

## What You Should Not Submit

Please do not submit:

- content copied from official films, books, games, promotional material, or other third-party sources
- ripped, extracted, traced, or upscaled copyrighted assets
- logos, marks, branding, or artwork that you do not have permission to contribute
- material taken from other resource packs, mods, websites, or repositories unless you have clear permission to use it
- anything you do not personally have the right to contribute

If there is uncertainty about whether an asset is appropriate to include, please mark it clearly in the PR for maintainers to review.

## Pull Request Expectations

When opening a pull request:

- describe the change clearly
- keep unrelated changes out of the same pull request
- use clear filenames and organization
- note version compatibility where relevant
- explain any technical limitations if needed

## Screenshots and Public-Facing Material

Screenshots used in pull requests, documentation, or other public-facing materials may be edited, replaced, or removed by the maintainers.

The maintainers may decline screenshots or promotional material that are unsuitable for public display.

1. you created the contribution yourself, or you otherwise have the legal right to submit it;
2. your contribution does not knowingly infringe the rights of any third party;
3. you have the right to allow the project maintainers to use, modify, adapt, display, distribute, and maintain your contribution as part of this project.

By submitting a contribution, you grant the project maintainers a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable license to use, reproduce, modify, adapt, publish, distribute, and otherwise use that contribution as part of the project and its related repositories, releases, documentation, and promotional materials.

You also agree that the maintainers may refuse, edit, remove, or stop using any contribution at any time.

## No Ownership Transfer of Third-Party IP

Submitting a contribution does not transfer or create any ownership in any third-party intellectual property that may be referenced by the project. All third-party rights remain with their respective owners.

## Maintainer Review

All contributions are reviewed manually.

A submission may be closed or rejected for technical, artistic, organizational, or legal reasons, including uncertainty about asset origin or rights status.

## Conduct

Be respectful and constructive in pull request discussions.
