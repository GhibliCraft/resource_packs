Welcome to GhibliCraft, a project dedicated to recreating the enchanting worlds of Studio Ghibli in Minecraft! Our goal is to bring the unique artistry and magical atmospheres of Ghibli films into the Minecraft universe, providing players with an immersive experience like no other.

## About the Project

GhibliCraft is a collaborative effort among passionate Minecraft players, resource pack creators, and modelers who share a deep love for the enchanting worlds created by Studio Ghibli. By combining the iconic visual style of Ghibli films with the endless possibilities of Minecraft, we aim to transport players into a realm of wonder and nostalgia.

### Project Features

- Faithful recreation of Ghibli film locations within Minecraft
- Custom resource pack and textures inspired by Ghibli's artistry
- Models and structures meticulously designed to capture Ghibli's magical essence
- Continuously expanding world with new updates and additions

## Getting Started (@Visitors)

Welcome to GhibliCraft! While only GhibliCraft staff members have direct access to the repository, there are still several ways you can participate and stay connected:

1. **Explore Our Work**: You can check out the [YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/AlanBeckerMinecraft) for breathtaking showcases of our creations or visit our [Blog](https://www.ghiblicraft.com) for in-depth insights into the development process.

2. **Join Our Community**: If you're a Minecraft enthusiast or a Studio Ghibli fan, consider joining our vibrant Discord community at [GhibliCraft Community](https://discord.gg/ghiblicraft). Here, you can share your love for GhibliCraft, discuss ideas, and interact with like-minded individuals.

3. **Support and Feedback**: If you're impressed with our project or have suggestions to share, don't hesitate to drop us a message on Discord or leave feedback on our YouTube videos. Your support and feedback mean the world to us!

## Getting Started (@Builders & @Staff)

As a GhibliCraft builder, you have the privilege of contributing to the project's development. Follow these steps to access and collaborate:

1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/your-username/GhibliCraft.git`
2. Install Minecraft and make sure you have the latest version.
3. Review the contribution guidelines in the [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) file to get familiar with our workflow and standards.
4. Explore the project structure, resources, and ongoing discussions in the repository to find areas where you can contribute your skills and ideas.
